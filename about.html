About Us


Perm Recruitment was established in 2021 and quickly earned a reputation as a trustworthy and dependable recruitment partner for permanent recruitment solutions in Ireland.


Today we are the recruitment partner of choice for thousands of jobseekers and employers across Ireland.

At any given time, we have an active candidate pool of over 50,000 jobseekers and hundreds of companies, small and large, across a range of sectors.

What we offer


We offer both permanent long-term solutions; from It specialists needed to meet a deadline or cover busy periods, through to long-term placements and sole supplier retained contracts.


The team at Perm Recruitment have experience in Ireland, the UK, Europe and globally, and this, coupled with our commitment to listening precisely to your needs, and our consistent placement of quality candidates with quality companies, has been the key to our success.
